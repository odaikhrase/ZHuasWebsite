

# Decor Emperor — Premium Luxury Website

A modern, luxury website for Decor Emperor LLC featuring premium Marble PVC Wall Panels and WPVC decorative sheets in Columbus, Ohio. Dark/light contrast design with gold accents, smooth scroll animations, and mobile-first responsive layout.

---

## Design System
- **Color palette**: Deep charcoal (#1a1a1a), off-white (#f5f0eb), gold accents (#c9a84c), warm brown (#3d2b1f)
- **Typography**: Elegant serif for headings (Playfair Display), clean sans-serif for body (Inter)
- **Style**: Generous whitespace, marble-texture backgrounds, subtle gold dividers, fade/slide-on-scroll animations, tasteful hover effects

---

## Page 1: Home
- **Top bar**: Decor Emperor logo + brand name, phone icon (links to call), email link (info@decoremperor.com)
- **Navigation**: Home · Products · Gallery · Shipping & Pickup · Contact
- **Hero section**: Full-width hero with marble panel background image, headline "Luxury Marble PVC Wall Panels", subheadline quote from the site, two CTAs ("Explore Marble PVC Panels" / "Explore WPVC Sheets"), secondary CTA "Get a Quote"
- **What We Offer section**: Descriptive paragraph about marble PVC panels and WPVC sheets for Columbus, Ohio — with the two product images from the site side by side
- **Why Choose Decor Emperor**: 6 elegant feature cards with gold icon accents — Luxury Marble Appearance, Waterproof & Long-Lasting, Fast Simple Installation, Luxury Without the High Cost, Large Panel Size (9 ft × 4 ft, 8 mm), Advanced WPVC Decorative Panels
- **Panel Specs highlight**: A visual callout block showcasing the 9 ft × 4 ft size and 8 mm thickness
- **Recent Installations**: Horizontal carousel slider showing all 8 installation photos from the site, with a "More Images" button linking to the Gallery page
- **Shipping & Pickup teaser**: Brief callout — "Pickup only from our facility in Columbus, Ohio" with link to full policy page
- **Footer**: Brand statement, contact info (email, business name), navigation links, copyright

## Page 2: Products
- **Section A — Marble PVC Wall Panels**: Product description text from the site, image grid featuring marble panel product photos and installation images, the full installation guide (8 steps) presented as an elegant accordion
- **Section B — WPVC Sheets / Decorative Panels**: Description from the site about wood-plastic composite strength, image grid with WPVC-related photos
- **Sticky "Request Pricing" CTA**: Fixed button that scrolls/navigates to the Contact page form

## Page 3: Gallery
- **Masonry image grid**: All gallery images from the site (the extended gallery page with ~20+ images) displayed in a responsive masonry layout
- **Lightbox view**: Click any image to open a full-screen lightbox with navigation arrows
- **Simple layout** (no filters, as the site doesn't categorize images)

## Page 4: Shipping & Pickup
- **Full policy page**: The exact shipping & pickup policy text from the site
- **Pickup Location callout**: Prominent card highlighting "Pickup Only — Columbus, Ohio"
- **CTA**: "Have questions? Contact us" linking to Contact page

## Page 5: Contact
- **"Get In Touch" section**: Headline and supporting copy from the site — "Ready to transform your space with premium marble PVC panels?"
- **Contact form**: First name, Last name, Email (required), Message (required), Phone — with client-side validation (form submits via mailto or displays a success message; no backend needed initially)

## Interactions & Animations
- Fade-in on scroll for all sections
- Subtle parallax effect on hero background image
- Hover scale effects on product cards and gallery images
- Smooth page transitions between routes
- Carousel with touch/swipe support for Recent Installations
- Lightbox with keyboard navigation in Gallery

## Technical Details
- All images sourced directly from decoremperor.com (Wix static URLs)
- React Router for multi-page navigation
- Fully responsive mobile-first design
- SEO meta tags, page titles, and OpenGraph tags per page
- No backend required — contact form will show a success toast (can be connected to email service later)
